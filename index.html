About

I’m a Norwegian goldsmith based in Barcelona with a background in glassblowing and contemporary craft. After years traveling and working as a glass artist — exploring form, movement and light — I turned my attention to jewelry: the same obsession with material at a smaller scale. My approach blends bohemian chic with Scandinavian clarity — organic forms, textured surfaces and durable pieces meant to be worn every day.

Services include repairs & polishing, custom commissions and a curated selection of pre-made pieces. Every piece is handled personally in the Barcelona workshop.
